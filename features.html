Under the Hood

GeekHub isn't just a database. It's a compatibility engine designed to save you money and maximize frames. Here is how our technology works.

Core Technology

Smart GPU Matching Algorithm

Our proprietary matching logic doesn't just look at price. It analyzes the specific render pipelines of the games you select.

  • Rasterization vs. Ray Tracing: If you select Cyberpunk 2077, we prioritize cards with dedicated RT cores. For CS2, we prioritize high clock speeds.
  • VRAM Calculation: We sum the texture requirements of your target resolution (4K vs 1080p) to ensure you never run out of video memory.
System Health

Bottleneck Detection

Putting a Ferrari engine in a sedan doesn't make it a racecar. We prevent you from overspending on a GPU that your CPU cannot keep up with.

GPU Bound
